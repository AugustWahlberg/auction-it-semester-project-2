Q & A

Q: What happens to my credit after I have made a bid, and and another user bids over me?

A: Your credit will be returned within 15 minutes

Q: I am trying to make a bid on a listing, but the option is disabled?

A: Only logged in users can make a bid on a listing, you can login here

Q: Can I make a bid higher than my credit?

A: No, that is not possible

Q: How can I get more credit?

A: You can get more credit by listing items, it is not possible to deposit credits.
